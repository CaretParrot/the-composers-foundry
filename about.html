About

The Composers Foundry is a community dedicated to helping people of all ages, abilities, and backgrounds get involved in the world of music composition, theory, and analysis. From medieval gregorian chant to modern pop music, we hold discussions on a wide variety of genres of music. If you are looking to deepen your knowledge on music through analysis sessions, or if you are looking for some inspiration to start on a work, this is the place for you!

If you are interested in joining, please join our Discord server and follow our Instagram.
