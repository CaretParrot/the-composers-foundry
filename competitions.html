April-May 2025: Villain

Welcome to the first composition competition hosted by The Composers Foundry. The theme for this first one is "villain". You are free to interpret what that means in the context of music however you would like, assuming that it is communicated effectively through the music alone. The deadline for submissions is May 31st, 2025, at 11:45 PM. Please go to the top of the page to submit your score and audio file.
